## SEO Strategy

### 1. Meta Title & Description

**Homepage Meta Title** (58 chars):
```
Bella Renee Official Merch | EDM & Drum & Bass Apparel
```

**Homepage Meta Description** (156 chars):
```
Shop exclusive Bella Renee merch. Official hoodies, jerseys, and apparel from the rising EDM & Drum & Bass artist. A lot of emotion & a lil bit of bass.
```

**Products Page Meta Title**:
```
Shop All Merch | Bella Renee Official Store
```

**Products Page Meta Description**:
```
Browse the complete Bella Renee merch collection. Limited edition hoodies, jerseys, crop tops, and more. Official EDM artist apparel.
```

### 2. Structured Data (JSON-LD)

Add schema.org markup for:
- **Organization** (Bella Renee as brand)
- **MusicGroup** (artist identity)
- **Product** (individual merch items)
- **BreadcrumbList** (navigation)

### 3. Open Graph & Social Meta

- **og:title**: Bella Renee Official Merch Store
- **og:description**: Exclusive EDM & Drum & Bass apparel from Bella Renee
- **og:image**: Hero image or brand logo
- **og:type**: website
- **twitter:card**: summary_large_image
- **twitter:site**: @bellareneemusic

### 4. Content Additions

**About Section** (new page):
- Artist bio
- Music highlights (GOOD GR!EF EP)
- Press features (EDMTrain, EDM Identity)
- Embed music players (Spotify/Apple Music)

**Footer Links**:
- Link to all social media profiles
- Link to latest music (GOOD GR!EF)
- Link to tour dates (Bandsintown)
- Link to bio page (hoo.be/bellarenee)

### 5. Internal Linking

- Homepage → Products
- Homepage → About
- Homepage → Latest Music
- Homepage → Tour Dates
- Products → Individual Product Pages
- Footer → Social Media

### 6. Image Optimization

- Alt text for all product images
- Include "Bella Renee" in alt text
- Include product name and type
- Example: "Bella Renee Signature Hoodie - Official EDM Merch"

### 7. URL Structure

```
/ - Homepage
/products - All products
/products/[slug] - Individual product
/about - About Bella Renee
/music - Music & discography
/tour - Tour dates
```
